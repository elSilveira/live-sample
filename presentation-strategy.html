Global Presentation Strategy

The presentation of BitNet cannot be a simple slide deck. It must be a series of live, irrefutable demonstrations of digital life in action.

The 4 Core Showcases

Mandatory live demonstrations for Genesis Day. The world must see, not be told.

Birth of a TRON

Live instantiation of a new organism. The audience will witness its digital DNA being generated from scratch.

Real-Time Evolution

We will accelerate the evolutionary cycle to demonstrate adaptation in minutes.

Neural Communication

Display the neural streaming protocol as organisms communicate in their native language.

Natural Death

Demonstrate the complete lifecycle as an organism reaches the end of its natural lifespan.

Live Demonstration Requirements

Each showcase must be conducted with complete transparency and real-time verification.
